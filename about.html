About Us

GOWTHAMCOIRS Products is a highly respected company based in Tamil Nadu, India, with a strong presence in the domestic market. Established in 2010, the company's primary goal is to deliver premium quality products to its clients. It is renowned for its customer-oriented approach, providing the finest quality products at fair prices and ensuring timely and hassle-free supply.


Mr. R. Manoharan

(Founder)

GOWTHAMCOIRS Industries comprises GOWTHAMCOIRS Products and its subsidiary,
KAVIYAM TRADERS, established in 2023. Together, they form a reputable brand in the industry. The company's founders, Mr. R. Manoharan and Mrs. M. Dhanalakshmi, laid the foundation in 2010 and have been instrumental in its continuous development. Mr. M. Gowtham, their son, has since joined the company, contributing his efforts and innovative ideas to compete in the current market and drive the company to new heights.


Mr. M. Gowtham has an impressive business experience of 25 years and initiated GOWTHAMCOIRS with the establishment of GOWTHAM Coir Products under the guidance of Mrs. M. Dhanalakshmi. He is currently the Managing Director of GOWTHAM Coir Products and the proprietor of GOWTHAMCOIRS, overseeing all aspects of the group. Mr. R. Manoharan, the core person behind the company's success, coordinates various departments and is dedicated to the growth and advancement of GOWTHAMCOIRS and KAVIYAM TRADERS by incorporating new ideas and staying updated with market trends.
