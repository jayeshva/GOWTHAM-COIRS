ABOUT OUR BEST

Gowtham Coir Industry is a trusted and reliable source for high-quality coir-based products. With a commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility, the company is a leader in the industry and continues to innovate and expand its product offerings to meet the changing needs of customers around the world.
We manufacture 100% Natural and Organic Coir Products to the horticulture, floriculture & substrates Industry. CoirNest Coir is used worldwide as a growing medium for various hydroponic crops, nurseries, plant propagation and bedding plants.
